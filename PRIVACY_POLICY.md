# Privacy Policy for Flux Recorder

**Last Updated:** December 29, 2024

## Introduction

Flux Recorder ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our screen recording application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Flux Recorder operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personal data
- Track user behavior
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Share information with third parties

## Permissions Used

Flux Recorder requires the following permissions to function:

### Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
- **Purpose:** Enables the optional facecam feature during screen recording
- **Usage:** Only activated when you explicitly enable the facecam feature
- **Storage:** Camera footage is stored locally on your device
- **Sharing:** Never transmitted or shared externally

### Other Permissions
- **Record Audio:** To capture system audio and/or microphone input during recordings
- **Storage Access:** To save your recordings to your device's storage
- **Foreground Service:** To maintain recording while the app is in the background
- **Post Notifications:** To display recording status notifications

## Data Storage

All recordings and app data are stored locally on your device in:
- `/Movies/FluxRecorder/` directory
- App's internal storage for temporary files

You have full control over your recordings and can delete them at any time through:
- The app's recordings manager
- Your device's file manager
- System settings

## Third-Party Services

Flux Recorder does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Flux Recorder does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app does not collect any personal information from any users.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:
- We recommend using device encryption
- Set up screen lock protection
- Regularly back up important recordings

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All recordings are stored locally on your device
- You can delete any recording at any time
- You can uninstall the app to remove all associated data
- No data is retained by us after uninstallation

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email:** support@fluxrecorder.com
